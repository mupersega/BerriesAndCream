Berries and Cream

Game Info

This is a semi-cozy game all about managing your little lads who like berries and cream.

Please note, this game is in active development and that you may track my progress and future plans in the development tab.

The Goal

Have fun and manage your little lads. Forage for berries, Fetch cows for cream, Fell trees for lumber, track your current resources in the top right. Your currently available little lads can be seen in the little lads window to the left of the screen.

Here you will find their available behaviours, gain more info as to their inventory and current pathing information by pressing the chevron to the right of behaviours.

For example, to forage for berries you will need to do the following:

  1. Select a tile near to a berry pile, berry bush, or berry tree,
  2. Designate tiles around the selected tile to be forageable by pressing the 'Forage' button in the top bar,
  3. Set one of your little lad's behaviour to 'Forage' by pressing the berry icon,
  4. Watch as your little lads forage berries, and return them to their home.
